Welcome to AGRICARE

Enhancing productivity and sustainability in agriculture.

Agricare is dedicated to empowering farmers through advanced agricultural extension services. We provide expert guidance and support to help farmers maximize their yield and achieve sustainable farming practices. We are dedicated to providing top-notch agricultural extension services to farmers, helping them improve productivity and sustainability.

  • Our Mission
We aim to improve the lives of farmers by providing innovative solutions, continuous education, and resources that drive efficiency, sustainability, and growth in the agricultural sector. To establish an efficient and demand-driven extension service in a decentralized system through partnership between the government and the private sector for provision of quality service to our clients.
  • Our Vision
Since its founding, Agricare has been at the forefront of providing essential agricultural services to smallholder and commercial farmers alike. Our journey started with a mission to address the pressing challenges of modern agriculture, offering hands-on support and solutions. Today, we continue to be a leading force, transforming agricultural practices and enriching the lives of those who grow the food that nourishes us all.
